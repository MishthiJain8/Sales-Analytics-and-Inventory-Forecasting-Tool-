## Sample CSV Format

Already included, but here's the format:

```
Date,Product,Revenue,Inventory
2024-01-01,Laptop,15000,45
2024-01-01,Mouse,2500,120
2024-01-02,Keyboard,4800,85
2024-01-03,Monitor,8500,60
```

**Flexible column names work:**
- `Date` or `date`
- `Product` or `product` 
- `Revenue` or `Sales`
- `Inventory` or `Stock`
